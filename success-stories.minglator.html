Challenge

  • High Price

    KUBOTA provides a high quality of product and long-lasting but comes with a quite high price when compared with competitors in the market.

  • Product Confidence

    Following concern about the price, customers still have a question about the long-lasting use & product warranty after they buy.

  • Unaware of Brands

    Myanmar people still don’t know that Toyota cars are being sold in the country. Including the use of service centers is still relatively low.

What We Did ?

  • Awareness

    Build brand awareness & recognition by organizing events often in the mall. In order to get to know the brand as well as to group people who want to be customers.

  • Partnership

    Cooperate with Mitsui (Japan) to find out and quickly access information. And create new local partners such as gas stations, shops etc. for marketing.

  • Marketing

    Use a referral program to draw people & new customers using the service from the recommendation. And including using partners to boost sales & obtaining marketing information in the future.

  • Operational Excellence

    Know & understand what the problem is. And know what to do in the next step to resolve the problem. By working in “Operational Excellence”, the work processes in the company are improved, faster, more economical, safer by focusing on efficiency & effectiveness.

  • Brand Monitoring

    Use data monitoring in all aspects, whether it’s a working style, market movements. The company has been able to provide “in-depth” market information and other aspects of its operations.
